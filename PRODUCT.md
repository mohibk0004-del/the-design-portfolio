# Product Context

**Brand / Product Name:** The Design Portfolio (Mohib)
**Users:** Recruiters, hiring managers, other developers, and creative directors.
**Product Purpose:** A premium, interactive portfolio showcasing Mohib's skills in Video Editing, Software Engineering, 3D, and Frontend Design. It must serve as proof of exceptional taste and technical execution.
**Register:** brand
**Vibe / Tone:** Highly polished, hardware-accelerated, slightly experimental but extremely premium. Uses 3D elements, immersive scroll physics, and clean typography.

**Key Principles:**
- Unseen details compound to create the overall premium feel.
- Beauty is leverage. The design differentiates the engineer.
- Motion must be purposeful and physically grounded.
